I found myself once again tasked to create, plan and complete a self-directed project for the whole semester (a shorter one this time). At first, I struggled to decide whether I wanted to take my Blackpink project further or to start a new one.

After a week of contemplation, I came across a video that would steer this whole project. And that video was the McDonalds UK advert directed by Edgar Wright. I was entertained by his filming style as I’ve enjoyed his movies in the past. What piqued my interest especially was that he managed to create an incredible commercial using the distinct mixture of his style of filming and music choice, all without showing a single image of the product. Relying only on subtle hints on colour and a reimagined way of symbolizing the McDonalds arches, Edgar Wright delivered a fresh, new take on commercials.

Inspired by this advert, I decided to combine two things that I enjoy: video production & Pokémon GO.
